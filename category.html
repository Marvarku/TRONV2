Our Sustainability Roadmap

Committed to a sustainable future with clear, measurable goals.

2025

Net Zero Operations

Achieve carbon neutrality across all TRON operations and facilities.

  • 100% renewable energy for all offices
  • Zero waste to landfill
  • Carbon offset programs
2030

Fleet Electrification

Complete transition to 100% electric vehicle fleet across all markets.

  • 10,000+ electric buses deployed
  • Charging infrastructure network
  • Smart grid integration
2050

Climate Positive

Go beyond net zero to become climate positive, actively removing carbon from the atmosphere.

  • Carbon capture technology
  • Reforestation initiatives
  • Circular economy leadership
